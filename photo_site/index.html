Story

Content-creating has been a meaningful part of my life outside of my professional background. In my spare time, I work on poster, multi-media painting and photography.

I primarily focus on nature photography, capturing colours, special spices of plantes, and expressing beauty and imagination through my lens. I also enjoy shooting/painting about landscapes and architecture — I find inspiration in both people and the world around them.

Back Light

Everything reflects some special colour when the sunlight on it's back. I love how the creator of the universe designed humaned eyes that is much more advanced in seeing vivid colours even there's strong light on the back. The best digital camera won't capture the same amount of colour resolution than our bare eyes.

Architecture

Colours

And yes, this is another section of nature. I was attracted by the abandant vatility in plantes, trees and natures.Even though they don't work to buy new clothes, they have the most pure colour that the luxury brand of fashion can never mimic and produce the same of how they grown to be. The flowers are pretty, but the leaves are prettier!

Nature

Influenced by the Asian concept of "changing scenery with each step," I often find myself encountering nature's own canvas while walking through national parks — a symphony composed of living and dead trees, shrubs, and creeks. The entire sky seems to proclaim the glory and design of the Creator.

City Walk

Painting w/ mixed multi-media

I have a deep love for oil painting, acrylics, pastel, and watercolor. Impressionism is one of the art movements I admire most, and I enjoy adding my own unique artistic touches to this style. Rather than replicating real-life scenes, I prefer creating dreamlike landscapes.

Additional Information about Author

Finally you are here, congrats that you have a glimps of all my works. Here's some extra works that might not be artistic but have specail meanings for me personnally. I hope you will enjoy this section to know more about me :D

Why photography?

I was grown up with a doctor family, and for my whole life i've been taught to study Bio in order to get into a med school. I followed the instruction and live for fill the expectation of parents. My career in health It's quite success until I got admit by my cejep the Marianopolis college, which has the best private health program in Montreal. However, this is also my at this beautiful and rigorous school, I encountered the greatest setback in my journey of biological professions, and it ended up in becoming the turning point in my life decision. I started by interested to do PhotoShop digital edition in my young age, and then I graduaally get into interests in presenting as a model and produce some dramatic photoes. Yet later on, I graduatlly became more assure what is the output I want to produce. with a tons of thought of the meanings and messages I want to express, I find that being an model is so restricted, because the style I could modeling is limited and being a model means becoming the object of aesthetics. What I long for is more freedom to record and actively capture moments of beauty, rather than merely appreciating my own beauty within the expressive style I am already good at. Because of this shift in mindset, I began studying the theory of light and shadow, and fell in love with the style of film. have always believed that if a gift is not used to its fullest, it is wasted ---- and I don’t like waste! In the future, I will continue to use the black eyes God has given me to search for light, and to capture with my camera the shapes of light and the fleeting moments of shadow and illumination. When I press the shutter, am I recording a slice of eternity? In some sense, is the instant also eternal? I don’t have the answer to that question, but I know I will keep searching till the day I left this world.
